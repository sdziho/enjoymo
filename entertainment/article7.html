Fortica Mountain Lodge

Spend pleasant moments in nature near the city. Fortica Mountain Lodge offers accommodation in a natural setting just 5 km from the city center and can be your starting point for exploring the local mountains or city sights. Accommodation capacities are divided into two bedrooms with 6 beds each and a shared bathroom and toilet. Guests have at their disposal free parking, WI-FI and use of all the facilities of the mountain lodge (kitchen, outdoor terrace, barbecue, etc.) In the mountain lodge you can get instructions for organizing excursions or enjoy one of our offers (zipline, Via Ferata, climbing, hiking, cycling, etc.).
