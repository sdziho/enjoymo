Bunica boating

The offer includes a boat ride lasting 1h approx. , As well as Bosnian coffee for each person and homemade seasonal fruits. The boat on which we provide services is very strong, safe and adapted to Herzegovinian conditions (summer sun and winter winds). It is built of quality wood and its carrying capacity is up to 10 people. Due to the length and width of the water pressure, it is very heavy and stable. It is our Bosnian product. When you want to relax safety is inevitable.
