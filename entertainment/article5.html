ENTERTAINMENT

Mount biking

In the wider area of the Fortica Park as well as the Velež mountain, over 200 km of trails ideal for mountain biking have been marked, which you can tour alone or with our guides. At the mountain lodge you can get a free copy of your pocket card with all the marked trails, and bicycles are available for rent. A special offer is the rental of electric bicycles. There are various trails available from easy for beginners and children to extreme for experienced cyclists eager for adventure.
