ENTERTAINMENT

Zip Line

Experience an unforgettable descent down the ZIP LINE with a total length of 1000 m with a beautiful view of Mostar and the surrounding area. There are two cables 570 and 430 m long that connect the two elevations above Mostar, and we also offer children's ZIP LINE length 20 and 50 m. All users have at their disposal complete protective equipment from top world manufacturers, and before the descent they undergo a short training in the use of equipment. The maximum speed achieved during the descent is 90 km / h depending on the wind and the weight of the user. located at the state flag of BiH, and after the descent, a return to the starting point is ensured. Our experienced instructors will make sure that your descent is a safe and fun experience, and in addition to the individual descent, we also offer the ZIP LINE tandem.
