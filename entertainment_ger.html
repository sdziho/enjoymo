Ride into the cave

For many, a special attraction is a boat ride through the cave at the source of the Buna. Vrelo Bune is the largest karst spring in Europe in terms of water, with 43 cubic meters of water coming out of the spring in one second. It is specific for drinking water at the spring itself, and also on its longitudinal part further.
