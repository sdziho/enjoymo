The mevlud in Blagaj

The tradition of annual gatherings and annual farewells in Blagaj lasted until 1925, and in the same year her last sheikh died . Since then, the tekke has more or less become just a tourist attraction, but tourism between the two wars has not received enough attention either. However, the tradition of the Blagaj mevlud, colloquially known as the dervish mevlud, was renewed in 1975 by Sheikh Fejzulah ef Hadžibajrić - on behalf of the Tariqat Center, and the Mufti of Mostar h. Seid ef Smajkić - on behalf of the institutions of the Islamic Community in BiH. The traditional mevlud, according to the calendar of the Riyaset of the Islamic Community in BiH, is organized every other Saturday in May. This tradition has become an internationally significant event that brings together about 30,000 believers from Bosnia and Herzegovina and various parts of the world.
